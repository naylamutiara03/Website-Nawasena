BOOTH EXHIBITION

An exhibition booth is a space to showcase products or services at an event. It helps businesses attract attention, build brand awareness, and connect with customers. Nawasena is here to bring your ideas to life with creative designs and high-quality materials, ensuring a booth that meets your needs perfectly.

CUSTOM PRODUCTION

An event is a gathering or celebration designed to deliver a message or commemorate an important occasion, whether formal or informal, big or small. Nawasena is here to bring your event to life with thoughtful concepts, high-quality materials, and a professional team to ensure everything goes as planned.

RENTAL EQUIPMENT

Rental equipment refers to the leasing of essential items for events, such as acrylic podiums, sofas, chairs, tables, air conditioners, air coolers, and air purifiers. Nawasena provides a complete range of event equipment to support all your event needs.

SUPPORT ALL PRODUCT & CONCEPTS

Nawasena is here to support all your event needs by providing essential equipment such as tables, chairs, barstools, air conditioners, generators, sound systems, and LED videotrons. From concept and creative ideas to production and equipment, we ensure everything is covered for a successful event.
